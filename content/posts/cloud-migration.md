### The Vendor Lock-In Trap: Why It Matters for SMBs

Vendor lock-in occurs when businesses become so integrated with a provider's ecosystem that migrating away feels impossible. Proprietary APIs, custom configurations, and specialized services create "gray matter" complexities—those intricate, often undocumented dependencies that require deep expertise to unravel. For SMBs, this translates to higher costs, longer downtimes, and reluctance to explore cost-saving alternatives.

Traditional migrations involve manual audits, code rewrites, and extensive testing, often ballooning expenses and timelines. According to industry reports, the average cloud migration project can take months and cost tens of thousands of dollars, deterring many from making the switch. But what if there was a way to slash these barriers? Enter REQtec's unique agentic solutions—intelligent, autonomous AI-driven systems that automate the heavy lifting, reducing both time and costs while promoting tech independence.

### Our Process: What to Expect

At REQtec, we believe in transparency and empowerment throughout the migration journey. Our structured process ensures a smooth transition while equipping you with the tools and knowledge for long-term independence. Below is an overview of what you can expect when partnering with us for your cloud migration.

- **Inquiry**: It all starts with your initial outreach. Whether through our website, email, or a call, share your current setup, pain points, and goals. We'll respond promptly to gather preliminary details and assess if our agentic solutions align with your needs.

- **Consultation**: In this phase, our experts conduct a free in-depth assessment. We analyze your infrastructure, identify lock-in risks, and propose a tailored migration plan. This includes cost estimates, timelines, and potential alternatives.

- **Deployment**: We kick off the hands-on work here. Using our agentic AI, we execute the migration in iterative cycles. Each iteration involves deploying portions of your environment, gathering feedback, and refining based on real-time insights. This agile approach minimizes disruptions and allows for adjustments on the fly.

- **QA (Quality Assurance)**: Post-deployment, we run comprehensive tests to ensure everything functions as expected. The system simulates loads, checks for edge cases, and verifies security compliance, addressing any issues before they impact your operations.

- **Evaluation**: Together, we review the outcomes against your objectives. Metrics like cost savings, performance improvements, and reduced downtime are measured. This step confirms the migration's success and highlights any final tweaks.

- **Exit (Enablement)**: True to our mission of destroying vendor lock-in, we focus on your independence. We provide full documentation, training sessions, and handover tools so you can manage the new setup entirely on your own. No ongoing fees or dependencies—you're empowered to evolve without us.

This process is designed to be collaborative, efficient, and customer-centric, typically spanning 2-6 weeks depending on complexity.
